What is broadband internet?

Broadband internet refers to high-speed internet access that is faster and more reliable than traditional dial-up connections, enabling faster downloads, streaming, and browsing

How does broadband internet work?

Broadband internet uses a high-speed connection to transmit data through a wide range of frequencies, delivering fast and reliable internet access for various online activities.

What is the difference between upload and download speeds?

Upload speed refers to the rate at which data is sent from your device to the internet, while download speed is the rate data is received from the internet to your device.

What factors can affect broadband speed?

Broadband speed can be affected by factors such as network congestion, distance from the provider, type of connection, equipment quality, and interference from other devices or structures.
